Getting to Bandung

By Shuttle Bus (Cititrans)

From Soekarno-Hatta Airport to Bandung:

Take Cititrans shuttle bus from the airport directly to Bandung (Pasteur area). The pool is located at Jl. Dr. Djunjunan No. 153, very close to recommended hotels like Rooms Inc. Hotel and Aston Pasteur Hotel.

  • Price: Rp. 210,000 per person
  • Departures: Every 15 minutes from 00:30 to 23:59 daily
  • Travel time: Approximately 3 hours
  • Capacity: 8 seats per shuttle bus

This is the most convenient option for direct transportation from the airport to Bandung.

By WHOOSH High-Speed Train

Route: Halim Station (Jakarta) â†’ Padalarang Station (Bandung)

Step-by-step process:

  1. From Soekarno-Hatta Airport, take DAMRI bus to Halim Station (Rp. 80,000, every hour, ~1 hour journey)
  2. Take WHOOSH train from Halim to Padalarang (Rp. 150,000 - 300,000, ~30 minutes)
  3. Take FREE feeder train from Padalarang to Bandung Station (~20 minutes, operates 05:40-20:20)
  4. Take taxi/Grab from Bandung Station to your hotel (Rp. ~50,000, ~15-20 minutes)
  • WHOOSH Schedule: 06:08 to 20:48, every 30 minutes to 1 hour
  • Total journey time: Approximately 2.5-3 hours
  • Advantage: Faster and more modern, but requires multiple transfers

Alternative: Kertajati International Airport

Kertajati International Airport (KJT) serves West Java with domestic and some international flights.

  • Located about 68 km from Bandung city center
  • Travel time: Approximately 2 hours by car or shuttle bus
  • Less crowded than Soekarno-Hatta Airport

Note: Husein Sastranegara Airport (BDO) in Bandung now has very limited service with few domestic flights.

Local Transportation in Bandung

Once in Bandung, use these transportation options:

  • Taxis: Blue Bird Group (most reliable)
  • Ride-hailing: Grab, Gojek (recommended - download apps before arrival)
  • Typical fares: Rp. 30,000 - 50,000 for trips within city center
  • Hotel shuttles: Check with your accommodation for availability

Visa Information

Indonesia offers various visa options for international visitors:
  • Visa-free entry (for eligible countries)
  • Visa on Arrival (VOA) - 30 days, extendable once
  • E-visa application before travel

Visa Requirements

Check your visa requirements on the Indonesian Immigration website.

For visa support letters, please contact: his2025@ideas-lab.org

Important Notes

  • Passport must be valid for at least 6 months
  • Proof of onward/return travel may be required
  • Visa regulations may change - check official sources

Accommodation Information

Recommended Hotels

We recommend the following hotels near the conference venue:

  • Aston Tropicana Hotel Bandung - 4-star hotel with modern facilities, located in the heart of Bandung. Features meeting rooms, restaurant, and spa. Distance to venue: approximately 20 minutes.
  • Aston Pasteur Hotel - Contemporary 4-star hotel in Pasteur area with easy access to shopping and dining. Approximately 15 minutes to the conference venue.
  • Botanika Nature Hotel - Boutique hotel with natural ambiance, featuring eco-friendly design and garden views. Located near Lembang, about 30-40 minutes from the venue.
  • Holiday Inn Bandung Pasteur - International chain hotel with reliable service, business facilities, and fitness center. About 15-20 minutes to Maranatha University.
  • Holiday Inn Express Bandung Dago - Budget-friendly option from the Holiday Inn family, located in Dago area with good access to universities and shopping areas. Approximately 10-15 minutes to venue.

Special rates for conference attendees may be available. Please contact the hotels directly and mention the HIS 2025 conference for potential discounts.

Popular Areas to Stay

  • Dago Area - Close to universities, shopping centers, and restaurants. Excellent choice for conference attendees.
  • Pasteur Area - Modern neighborhood with many hotels, close to the conference venue.
  • Braga Street - Historic area with colonial architecture and vibrant nightlife.
  • Setiabudi Area - Upscale neighborhood with shopping malls and international restaurants.
  • Riau Street (Jl. L.L.R.E. Martadinata) - Shopping and culinary hotspot with local flavor.
