Travel Information

Going to Bandung

By Air

Bandung can be reached via Husein Sastranegara International Airport (BDO), which serves domestic flights within Indonesia and some international flights from neighboring countries. For more international connections, travelers can arrive at Jakarta's Soekarno-Hatta International Airport (CGK) and continue to Bandung.

From Husein Sastranegara Airport, the city center is easily accessible by taxi or ride-hailing services, with a journey time of approximately 15-20 minutes depending on traffic conditions.

By Train

Regular train services connect Bandung to major Indonesian cities, particularly Jakarta, via the historic Bandung Railway Station. The journey from Jakarta takes approximately 3-3.5 hours on executive class trains, offering a scenic route through West Java's mountainous landscape.

By Bus

Several bus companies operate regular services to Bandung from various cities in Java. The Leuwipanjang Bus Terminal serves as the main gateway for inter-city buses. Premium bus services from Jakarta's various terminals to Bandung take approximately 3-4 hours depending on traffic conditions.

By Car

Bandung is accessible via the Cipularang toll road from Jakarta, which takes approximately 2-3 hours under normal traffic conditions. The city is also connected to other major cities in West Java through well-maintained highways.

For more information, please refer to the Indonesia Tourism Board.

Visa to Indonesia

Citizens from many countries can enter Indonesia through Visa on Arrival (VOA) or Visa-Free facilities. The standard VOA allows for a 30-day stay and can be extended once for another 30 days.

Please refer to the Indonesian Immigration website to confirm your visa requirements and eligibility for VOA or Visa-Free entry.
