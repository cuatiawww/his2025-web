HIS 2025 @ Bandung

Travel Information

Getting to Bandung

By High-Speed Rail (WHOOSH)

The new WHOOSH high-speed rail service connects Jakarta and Bandung in just 15-20 minutes:

  • Jakarta Station: Halim Station (East Jakarta)
  • Bandung Station: Tegalluar Station (Bojongsoang)
  • Multiple daily departures

From Tegalluar Station to the conference venue, take a taxi or ride-hailing service (approximately 30-40 minutes).

By Air

Bandung's Husein Sastranegara International Airport (BDO) serves domestic flights and select international routes. International visitors can also arrive at Jakarta's Soekarno-Hatta International Airport (CGK) and continue to Bandung via high-speed rail or other ground transportation.

The conference venue is approximately 20-30 minutes from Husein Sastranegara Airport by taxi or ride-hailing service.

By Regular Train

Regular train services connect Bandung Railway Station with major Indonesian cities:

  • Jakarta - Bandung: 3 hours (executive class available)
  • Multiple daily departures from Gambir Station (Jakarta)
  • From Bandung Station to venue: 15-20 minutes by taxi

Ground Transportation

Local transportation options in Bandung:

  • Taxis: Blue Bird Group (most reliable)
  • Ride-hailing: Grab, Gojek
  • Online taxi booking through apps
  • Hotel shuttle services (check with your accommodation)

Visa Information

Indonesia offers various visa options for international visitors:
  • Visa-free entry (for eligible countries)
  • Visa on Arrival (VOA) - 30 days, extendable once
  • E-visa application before travel

Visa Requirements

Check your visa requirements on the Indonesian Immigration website.

For visa support letters, please contact: conference@his2025.org

Important Notes

  • Passport must be valid for at least 6 months
  • Proof of onward/return travel may be required
  • Visa regulations may change - check official sources
