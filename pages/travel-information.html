HIS 2025 @ Bandung

Travel Information

Getting to Bandung

By High-Speed Rail (WHOOSH)

The new WHOOSH high-speed rail service connects Jakarta and Bandung in just 36-45 minutes:

  • Jakarta Station: Halim Station (East Jakarta)
  • Bandung Station: Padalarang Station (West Bandung)
  • Multiple daily departures

From Padalarang Station to the conference venue, take a taxi or ride-hailing service (approximately 15-20 minutes). This station is closer to the conference venue than Tegalluar Station.

By Air

Kertajati International Airport (KJT) is now the main international airport serving West Java. Located about 68 km from Bandung, it offers domestic flights and some international routes.

International visitors can also arrive at Jakarta's Soekarno-Hatta International Airport (CGK) and continue to Bandung via high-speed rail (WHOOSH) or other ground transportation.

Note: Husein Sastranegara Airport (BDO) in Bandung now has very limited service with few domestic flights.

From Kertajati Airport to Bandung city center takes approximately 2 hours by car or shuttle bus.

By Regular Train

Regular train services connect Bandung Railway Station with major Indonesian cities:

  • Jakarta - Bandung: 3-3.5 hours (executive class available)
  • Multiple daily departures from Gambir Station (Jakarta)
  • From Bandung Station to venue: 15-20 minutes by taxi

Ground Transportation

Local transportation options in Bandung:

  • Taxis: Blue Bird Group (most reliable)
  • Ride-hailing: Grab, Gojek
  • Online taxi booking through apps
  • Hotel shuttle services (check with your accommodation)

Visa Information

Indonesia offers various visa options for international visitors:
  • Visa-free entry (for eligible countries)
  • Visa on Arrival (VOA) - 30 days, extendable once
  • E-visa application before travel

Visa Requirements

Check your visa requirements on the Indonesian Immigration website.

For visa support letters, please contact: his2025@ideas-lab.org

Important Notes

  • Passport must be valid for at least 6 months
  • Proof of onward/return travel may be required
  • Visa regulations may change - check official sources

Bandung Weather

Bandung enjoys a cooler climate due to its elevation (768m above sea level).

Average temperature: 23°C (73°F)

Rainy season: November to April

Dry season: May to October

We recommend bringing a light jacket or sweater for evenings.
