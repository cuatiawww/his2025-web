About Maranatha Christian University

Maranatha Christian University is one of Indonesia's premier private Christian universities, located in the heart of Bandung city. Founded in 1965, the university has established itself as a leading institution for higher education in Indonesia, consistently delivering excellence in academic programs and research.

The university's modern facilities and strategic location make it an ideal venue for international conferences. With state-of-the-art conference halls, advanced technological infrastructure, and a beautiful campus environment, Maranatha provides the perfect setting for academic discourse and professional networking.

Getting to Maranatha

Maranatha Christian University is centrally located in Bandung and easily accessible through various transportation options:

From Husein Sastranegara International Airport:

The university is approximately 6 km from the airport. Take a taxi (about 20-30 minutes depending on traffic) or book a ride-hailing service like Grab or Gojek.

From Bandung Railway Station:

The campus is about 4 km from Bandung's main railway station. You can take a taxi, or use ride-hailing services (approximately 15-20 minutes).

From Jakarta via High-Speed Rail (WHOOSH):

Take the WHOOSH high-speed train from Halim Station (Jakarta) to Tegalluar Station (Bandung). The journey takes only 15-20 minutes.

  • Halim Station (Jakarta): WHOOSH Station integrated with Halim Perdanakusuma, East Jakarta
  • Tegalluar Station (Bandung): Located in Tegalluar, Bojongsoang, about 15-20 minutes to city center
  • From Tegalluar Station to Maranatha: Take a taxi or ride-hailing service (approximately 30-40 minutes depending on traffic)
From Jakarta via Regular Train:

Alternatively, you can take regular train services:

  • From Gambir Station to Bandung Station (3 hours journey)
  • From Bandung Station to Maranatha: 15-20 minutes by taxi or ride-hailing service
From Jakarta via Shuttle Services:

Direct shuttle services are available from various points in Jakarta (3-4 hours depending on traffic):

  • Travel services from Blok M, Kuningan, Cibubur, and other major areas in Jakarta
  • Popular shuttle services: Citi Trans, X Trans, Xtreme Shuttle

Local Transportation:
  • Taxis: Blue Bird Group, Grab Car
  • Motorcycle taxis: Grab Bike, Gojek
  • Public transportation: Angkot (public minivan) routes passing near the campus
