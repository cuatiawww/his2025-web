About EdUHK

The Education University of Hong Kong (EdUHK) is a publicly funded tertiary institution dedicated to the advancement of teaching and learning, through a diverse offering of academic and research programmes on teacher education and complementary social sciences and humanities disciplines.

EdUHK nurtures educators and social leaders who are intellectually active, socially caring, and globally aware, to become agents of change in the communities that they serve. EdUHK places great emphasis on research capability — research will contribute to the advancement of knowledge, scholarship and innovation, with sustainable impact on social progress and human betterment.

Getting to EdUHK

The Education University of Hong Kong is located in Tai Po, New Territories, Hong Kong. The campus is easily accessible by public transportation.

From Hong Kong International Airport to EdUHK:

Take a taxi to EdUHK direct (about HK$350-HK$400), or take Airbus E41 to Tai Po Centre then change to bus 74K or taxi (about HK$40)

From Shenzhen to EdUHK:

Take MTR train from Lo Wu to Tai Po Market Station then change to bus 74K (details) or taxi (about HK$40)

From Hung Hom MTR Station to EdUHK:

Take MTR train to Tai Po Market Station then change to bus 74K (details) or taxi (about HK$40)

For more transportation information, please refer to the EdUHK Visitors.
