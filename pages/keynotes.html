Prof. Dr. Wahyu Widowati, M.Si

Biomedicine and Molecular Biology Researcher

Talk Title: To Be Announced

Biography

Prof. Dr. Wahyu Widowati, M.Si, is a researcher and lecturer specializing in biomedicine, molecular biology, and regenerative medicine. She has published over 156 papers indexed in Scopus, with an H-index of 22 and more than 1,493 citations. In Google Scholar, she has 305 publications, an H-index of 34, and 4,903 citations. Additionally, in Web of Science (WOS), she has 71 documents with an H-index of 13.

Prof. Widowati has actively contributed in total of 23 funded research projects, including grants from PPTI, PTUPT, LPPM-UKM, and DIKTI. She also participated in 31 scientific seminars and conferences as an invited speaker, sharing insights on stem cell applications, herbal-based therapeutics, and biomedical advancements. She has also authored 13 books, in topics such as stem cells, antioxidants, phytotherapy, biomedical sciences, and anti-aging research.

In addition to publications, she holds total of 64 copyright, with 13 granted certificates and 51 in process. These copyrights primarily focus on biomedical research methodologies and herbal-based formulations. As an expert in the field, she has been an active reviewer for Scopus-indexed journals (Q1-4) since 2019 until now with a total of 410 reviewed papers, which contributes to scientific growth.
