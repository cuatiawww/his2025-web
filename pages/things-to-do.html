HIS 2025 @ Bandung

Things to Do in Bandung

Explore Bandung

Bandung, known as the "Paris of Java," offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, rich history, and modern attractions. During your visit for HIS 2025, take time to explore the city's stunning landscapes, vibrant culture, and delicious culinary scene.

Natural Attractions

Nature

Tangkuban Perahu

An active stratovolcano with stunning crater views. Walk around the rim and experience the volcanic landscape with sulfuric vents and hot springs.

2-3 hours 30 km north
Nature

Kawah Domas

Natural hot springs where you can boil eggs in volcanic water. A unique experience near Tangkuban Perahu volcano.

1-2 hours 32 km north

Historical & Cultural Sites

Heritage

Saung Angklung Mang Udjo

Cultural center showcasing traditional Sundanese music and bamboo angklung performances in a natural setting.

2 hours 8 km east
Museum

Museum Geologi Bandung

Geology museum featuring fossils, minerals, and volcanic exhibits. Learn about Indonesia's geological history.

1-2 hours City center
Heritage

Braga Street

Historic street with Art Deco buildings, cafes, and galleries. The heart of Bandung's colonial heritage.

1-2 hours Walkable
Museum

Asia Afrika Museum

Historical museum commemorating the 1955 Asian-African Conference, a milestone in Third World solidarity.

1 hour City center

Shopping & Entertainment

Shopping

Dago & Riau Factory Outlets

Famous shopping districts with numerous factory outlets offering branded clothing at discounted prices.

2-3 hours City area
Culinary

Tempayan Bistro

Explore local Sundanese cuisine including batagor, siomay, nasi timbel, and the famous Bandung cafes.

Varies Citywide

Travel Tips

  • Bring a light jacket - Bandung can be cool
  • Use Grab or Gojek for easy transportation
  • Try local Sundanese cuisine
  • Weekends are busier at attractions
  • Bargain at traditional markets
