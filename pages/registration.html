Registration Process

  1. Click the "Register Now" button above to fill out the online registration form
  2. Pay using one of the available methods:
    • Bank Transfer (Wire/TT) - via secure card gateway
    • Indonesian E-Wallets (QRIS) - OVO, GoPay, DANA, LinkAja
    • International Credit Card - for overseas participants
  3. Email the payment proof to icot2025@ideas-lab.org (include your name and paper ID)
  4. You will receive a confirmation email with your registration details
  5. For onsite participants, collect your conference kit at the registration desk

    Conference Venue

    Conference Venue

    Address: Universitas Kristen Maranatha, Jl. Prof. drg. Surya Sumantri No.65, Sukawarna, Kec. Sukajadi, Kota Bandung, Jawa Barat 40164, Indonesia

    About Universitas Kristen Maranatha

    Universitas Kristen Maranatha is one of Indonesia's premier private Christian universities, located in the heart of Bandung city. Founded in 1965, the university has established itself as a leading institution for higher education in Indonesia, consistently delivering excellence in academic programs and research.

    The university's modern facilities and strategic location make it an ideal venue for international conferences. With state-of-the-art conference halls, advanced technological infrastructure, and a beautiful campus environment, Maranatha provides the perfect setting for academic discourse and professional networking.

    Getting to Maranatha University

    Universitas Kristen Maranatha is centrally located in Bandung with easy access via various transportation options:

    From Recommended Hotels (Pasteur Area):

    Most recommended hotels (Aston Pasteur, Rooms Inc., etc.) are located near the Cititrans pool on Jl. Dr. Djunjunan No. 153 (Pasteur).

    • Distance to Maranatha: Approximately 5-7 km
    • Travel time: 15-20 minutes by taxi or Grab/Gojek
    • Estimated fare: Rp. 30,000 - 50,000
    From Bandung Train Station:

    If arriving via WHOOSH feeder train or regular train services:

    • Distance: Approximately 4 km from Bandung Station
    • Take a taxi or Grab/Gojek: 15-20 minutes, Rp. ~50,000
    From Padalarang Station (WHOOSH):

    Padalarang Station is the closest WHOOSH station to the conference venue:

    • Distance: Approximately 10-12 km
    • Recommended: Take taxi or Grab/Gojek directly to venue
    • Travel time: 20-25 minutes, Rp. 60,000 - 80,000
    • Alternative: Take free feeder train to Bandung Station, then taxi (adds 40 minutes total)
    Recommended Transportation Apps:
    • Grab: Download from App Store or Google Play (ride-hailing app)
    • Gojek: Local ride-hailing app with competitive prices
    • Blue Bird: Traditional taxi service, can be booked via phone or app
    • Tip: Download and set up these apps before arrival in Indonesia
