Registration Process

  1. Download the registration form here
  2. Fill out the form with your details
  3. Make the payment using one of the available methods
  4. Email the completed form and payment proof to registration@his2025.org
  5. You will receive a confirmation email with your registration details
  6. For onsite participants, collect your conference kit at the registration desk
